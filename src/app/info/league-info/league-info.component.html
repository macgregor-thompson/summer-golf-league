2018 Summer Golf League

Overview

The 2018 Summer Golf League will consist of 14 weeks of competition. There will be 3 teams of 4 players. Teams will be formed on a draft night at the captains' convenience. Play will consist of team and individual competitions. (see Formats below) All play will take place from the Maroon/Black (Combo) tee markers. Any potential rule changes will be discussed by the captains, but the head of the league will have the final say.

Head of the League

MacGregor Thompson

Captains

MacGregor Thompson, The Bird of Pars, The Man of Gan

League Schedule

Thursday May 17th through August 23rd No league play on Thursday May 31st since half of us will be in Scotland that week

Team Competition

The 2018 Summer Golf league will consist of 14 weeks of competition. The idea of this league is to get together with the boys one night a week during the summer, play a little golf and have some fun afterwards. The league is set up to make teams and pairings random, and it takes into consideration a players handicap to give everyone a fair chance of winning.
